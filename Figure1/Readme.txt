Data.txt: NRTI susceptibilitiy data downloaded from "https://hivdb.stanford.edu/download/GenoPhenoDatasets/NRTI_DataSet.txt".
Data.xlsx: Excel form of the data.
Data.mat: .mat form of the data.
Final.mat: Input-output data for six NRTI inhibitors.
Main.m: Produces Final.mat.
map.mat: Positions of 17 main isolates inside 1224 isolate vector.
Map_Code.m: Produces map.mat.
Muts_Rest.mat: Names of the 17 main isolates.
str_char_improved.m: Helps to produce Final.mat inside the Main.mat.
